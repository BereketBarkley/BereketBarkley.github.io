About Page

  • Our plan is to create a website that provides information on SHSAT schools offers to students by race group.
  • The race groups that we are focusing on are Asian, Black, LatinX, White, Multi-Racial, and Unknown.
  • We are organizing and analyzing data that we found on the State of New york Department for Eduaction website
  • Our plan:
  • 1. find a data set, upload a JSON page, and create an html
  • 2. organize the macro and micro page according to our prototype designs that we drew
  • 3. add data and facts, analyze and fulfill any uncompleted projects
  • Vocabulary:
  • SHSAT highschools: specialized NYC highschools for students who want to be challenged academically
  • Offers: If an SHSAT school offers admissions to a student, that is an offer
